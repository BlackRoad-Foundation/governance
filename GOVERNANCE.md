## Decision Making

| Level | Decision Type | Process |
|-------|--------------|---------|
| L1 | Day-to-day ops | Any core team member |
| L2 | Architecture changes | RFC + 2 approvals |
| L3 | Strategic direction | Alexa + founding team |
| L4 | IP/legal/financial | BlackRoad OS, Inc. board |

## RFC Process
1. Open issue in `BlackRoad-OS-Inc/blackroad-docs` with `[RFC]` prefix
2. Discussion period: 7 days minimum
3. Approval: 2 LGTM from CODEOWNERS
4. Implementation: assigned to agent or contributor
